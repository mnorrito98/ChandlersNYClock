Chandler's New Year's Eve Bash is here for the first year ever of fun and excitement! Be sure to arrive early for all of the festivities and the final countdown. The party starts on December 31 at noon with the PheobeRun 5K and 10K races. Sign up now or the morning of the race.

Family fun is available at the Hugsy's Bed Time Fun Palce with booths and free activities for the whole family, including performances from clowns, jugglers, and the moving childrens book readings by Joey Tribbiani.

The fun continues with A Taste of Tulsa with Monica from the culinary talents of Monica Geller and Tulsa's best eateries, and if you want an good place to hurl, the best place I can suggest to you would be at Rachel's English Triffle Stand located on Tulsa's downtown square.

Music from The Swing Kings and Ross's "Sound" brings us into the New Year. The dancing starts at 7 p.m. and continues until midnight. From 10 to 11 p.m. we say goodbye to the old year (and ring in the new) with the famous New Year's Eve Bash fireworks spectacular.
